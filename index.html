About Us

We are Natalie & Rebecca of The Teas Knees

A British tea room, celebrating the best in traditional and British home grown produce with a particular passion for the home brew and all its culinary accompaniments.

Currently operating as a ‘pop up’ we offer Classic Tea grown by the UK’s only producer, Tregothnan, and a vast selection of traditional cakes and cupcakes in your favourite past time flavours, not to mention the classic cream tea. All hand made with love, care and creativity using the best quality ingredients Britain has to offer.

Menu

Here is an example menu for a party package

Afternoon Tea

We can cater for any occasion with one of our superb afternoon tea menus. We can either arrange to deliver the package for self-service or supply staff to wait on you and your guests.

Scones

Freshly baked fruit scone served with Tiptree raspberry jam and a dollop of cornish clotted cream.

Sandwiches

Delicate finger sandwiches on a mixture of seeded, sourdough and wholemeal bread. We can offer a variety of fillings, including: Smoked Salmon, Cheddar & Chutney, Honey Roast Ham and Hummus & Red Pepper

  • Smoked Salmon, cream cheese and cucumber
  • Mature cheddar cheese and Red Onion Chutney
  • Honey Roast Ham and Ggrained Mustard Mayonnaise
  • Vegan Option: Hummus, Cucumber, Tomatoes and Sweet Red Pepper

Cakes

We can freshly bake complete cakes to be served by the slice. Various flavours on offer, including: Victoria Sponge, Lemon & Poppy Seed and Carrot Cake

  • Victoria Sponge
  • A classic and light vanilla sponge sandwich filled with fresh vanilla buttercream and a layer of Tiptree raspberry jam
  • Lemon and Poppy Seed
  • Fresh lemon sponge speckled with poppy seeds and topped with a zesty lemon drizzle
  • Carrot Cake
  • A favourite old timer two layered cake of moist carrot and walnut sponge filled and topped with a cream cheese icing and crunchy walnuts

Cupcakes

A variety of mini-cupcakes to fit in with the dainty feel. Flavours include: Banoffee Pie, Coconut & Lime and Bakewell Tart

  • Banoffee Pie
  • Vanilla sponge with crushed fresh bananna, a layer of caramel sauce, a squirt of vanilla buttercream icing and a crunchy flake in the top.
  • Coconut & Lime
  • Vanilla sponge made with coconut milk and flakes with a zesty fresh lime butter icing
  • Bakewell Tart
  • Almond flavoured sponge, filled with a spoon of Tiptree raspberry jam and topped with vanilla butter icing and a cheeky cherry

Tea

We can offer a variety of loose leaf teas to complement your choice of food, here are a few examples: Classic Tea, Earl Grey Blue Flower and Peppermint

  • Classic Black
  • Our Classic Black Tea is the only tea grown in the UK, blended with Assam and bought to you by Tregothnan based in Cornwall
  • Peppermint
  • Fairtrade organic mint leaf tea which is tingly, minty & fresh. Bought to you by Suki Tea
  • Earl Grey Blue Flower
  • Fairtrade organic Indian leaf tea with Centaurea Ceranus (cornflower petals) and natural Bergamot flavour bought to you by Suki Tea

Vintage Crockery Hire

Beautiful china tea sets

We have been furiously searching and hunting down hundreds of sets of bone china vintage cups and saucers in preparation for our tea room and have now got a huge and beautiful collection! Not only that we have managed to track down some gorgeous vintage tea pots, cake plates, milk jugs, sugar bowls and three tier cake stands which we are cataloguing and photographing as we write.

We would hate for these to be hidden away in storage so are planning to make it available for you to hire – perfect for any vintage wedding or afternoon tea party! Full details available soon...
